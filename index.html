THE CAPITAL
Jakarta
Jakarta is the most populous and the largest city in Indonesia. More than 9,608 million (2010) people are living there according to the UN data. Jakarta (DKI Jakarta) is located on the northwest of Java Island and bordering the Java Sea. People of Jakarta comes from different ethnicities: Javanese, Sundanese, Batak, Sulawesi, Madurese, Betawi, Malay, and Chinese are living there.
Jakarta's famous landmark is Monas Tower (National Monument Tower), located in Merdeka Square.
Kuala Lumpur
Kuala Lumpur or KL is the capital city of Malaysia. It is the largest city in Malaysia and the residence of the Malaysian King. Kuala Lumpur has a high english proficiency index considering this city is the home for different people and ethnic groups like Malay, Chinese, and Indian.
There are twin buildings/towers called Petronas Towers and it is the tallest twin buildings in this world.
Singapore
Singapore, which is both a city and a country, is one of the richest city in this world. This city is located in the south of Malaysia and the north of Batam Island (Indonesia). This city is the place for many ethnic groups. The largest groups are from China, Malaysia, and India. One of the official languages in Singapore is English and that makes this city has a very high english proficiency index.
Long years ago Singapore separated from Malaysia and became a Country. The first president is Yusof bin Ishak and the first prime minister is Lee Kuan Yew.
